Lectures

Typically you would give 2 hour presentation. The goals of lectures are acquiring knowledge and getting a content overview / getting the big picture. The online alternatives for lectures are designed with these goals in mind.

What to do

Offline activity we want to take online: a 2 hour lecture
Taking it online - Synchronous lecture
Do live Zoom lecture, maximum of 90 minutes content. Split in 3 x 30 minute blocks with break in-between.
Taking it online - Asynchronous lecture
  • Edit your previously recorded web-lectures or create short videos in which you explain the content. Aim for 45 min content. For example 3 x 15 min. videos with annotations.
  • Upload the video to CANVAS for viewing.
  • Provide a Discussion Board for student comments and add a probe question.
  • Conduct a 45-minute Zoom session with your students for reflection on the key aims of the lecture.
